About Us

Established in 2006, PRAISE Consultancy (Praise Business PLC) is a prominent consulting and training firm founded by competent and trust worthy professionals that have academic knowledge and well versed reputation in various fields and scopes.

PRAISE Consultancy provides a forum where the industry and service providers can meet and exchange knowledge and skills with the objective of filling the capacity gap that prevails in operating businesses and companies through consultancy, coaching, training and technology provision services addressing the needs of our customers.

For more than a decade the firm takeover an institution for a definite period and administers organizational change plans through our management lease modality. We have also proved to be effective in transformational leadership development, BSC, BPR, ISO management system, HR development, strategic planning management, marketing strategy, organizational structure management, resource management, performance management, customer handling mechanisms and other service by working with both governmental, private and NGOs.

Praise Consultancy business philosophy lies in the fact that companies and service users are partners. We highly appreciate the importance of mutual benefit through sharing of experiences in a two-way approach.
